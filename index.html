Master Thesis at Jayway

Designing for Motivation

Working with Anne Line Gjersem to explore how to design a VR environment that enhance users intrinsic motivation while performing reaction training. Following the Self Determination Theory through user-centered design developing a VR environment built to support autonomy & competence. This is a work in progress and is expected to be completed in June 2021.

Internal App for Tetra Pak

Low-fi Prototype

An assignment as a part of Tetra Pak Student Talent Program, developing a low fidelity prototype for internal Mobile App used by the operators when dealing with machine errors. The purpose was to communicate the ideas of the System Performance Management team regarding workflow and optimized searching.

AR Concept for Xocchiali

VR as a Powerful Tool for Prototyping

Working with Signe Jonsson and Daniella Jonsson to create a foundation for a natural, interactive user interface for AR glasses through research, principles for user-centered design and user tests. This project is made in cooperation with the AR start up Xocchiali. My main contribution involve building a VR environment in Unity to be used as a prototyping tool by simulating AR technology.

Environmental VR Game for Kids

Exploring Benefits of Empathy in VR

In an attempt to teach kids about caring for the environment, a VR game that takes place in the swedish mountains is developed. The project was performed together with three fellow students and my main contributions involve prototyping, 3D modelling, animation and project leading.

Interactive Fitness Application

A Functional Prototype

Creating a fitness application together with four fellow students. The app makes use of the local surroundings and features to complete workouts. The user is guided to new locations, instructed while performing exercises and the app registers the completion of them. The app emphasizes multimodal feedback for the cognitively demanding usage while on the go in an outside environment.
